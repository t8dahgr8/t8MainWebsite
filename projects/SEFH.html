2018 Science and Engineering Fair of Houston (LM386 vs PAM8403 Audio Efficiency Comparison)

LM386 • PAM8403 • Experiment

Overview

For SEFH 2018, I compared a classic class-AB amplifier (LM386) to a class-D module (PAM8403) to understand power efficiency at similar volume levels. I built circuits, measured electrical inputs versus the acoustic outputs, and presented results on the poster.

Highlights

  • Controlled measurements of input power and output SPL
  • Observed efficiency advantage of class-D PAM8403 vs LM386
  • Data logging and presentation
